The Community

Suburban living.
Urban convenience.

Inwood at Great Notch offers an easy lifestyle with recreational amenities designed for busy families — without the burden of time-consuming maintenance. The complex is ideally situated at the juncture of Clifton, Little Falls, Cedar Grove, and Montclair, adjacent to Garret Mountain Reservation.

Townhouses and apartment-style residences
Pool membership, tennis & basketball courts
Children's play areas throughout the grounds
Professionally managed community
Walk to NJ Transit commuter bus line

Getting Here

Conveniently connected to everything.

Nestled in suburbia but with the pulse of the metro right at your fingertips — Inwood is one of northern New Jersey's best-kept location secrets.

Lincoln Tunnel

15 minutes by car — one of the fastest commutes to Midtown Manhattan in all of NJ.

NJ Transit Bus

Walk directly to the NJ Transit commuter bus line — no car needed for your NYC commute.

NJ Transit Train

A short drive to the NJ Transit train line for additional commute options.

Garret Mountain

Adjacent to Garret Mountain Reservation — hiking, trails, and open green space steps away.
